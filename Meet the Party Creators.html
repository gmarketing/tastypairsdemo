Meet The Party Creators

Turning Food and Wine Pairing into a full blown taste explosion.

The Joy of sharing the magic of pairing wine and food in an interactive and entertaining manner was born in 2013.


Our unique concept takes the geeky, intellectual, learning part of the process and adds a little fun magic to it. making the part of molecular food and wine pairings an exciting game and adventure which translates them into simple bits that everyone can understand. 

 


We offer various tastings for corporate and private functions whether it be a birthday party, bachelorette party, wedding, golf day, product launch, team building or just a group of friends wanting to have fun and so something different.


We have qualified Cape Wine Masters that do the presentations and they are both informative and entertaining. 

Not only will you learn about the various tastings within the option you have chosen but hopefully you will also have knowledge that you can take away with you, that will help you with your choices in the future all while having fun.
